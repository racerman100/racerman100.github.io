Who We Are:

The Colby Woodsmen Team is a Co-ed Student Athletic Club. We Compete in timbersports events across the Northeast and Canada. We travel to a handful of meets each year in addition to hosting Mud Meet here at Colby. The club is open to any Colby Students interested, no experience necessary. All you need is a willingness to learn and a good sense of humor. If you’d like to get involved, feel free to come join us at practice any weekday from 4-6pm. We meet at the field across the road from the Hillside parking lot.


We also sell firewood to anybody interested! Contact our Treasurer -email in the contact page- for more information.
