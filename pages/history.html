Team History

The Colby Woodsmen Team has been around since 1950, when we first fielded a team. As best I can tell, Mud Meet runs back to 2007, when it was introduced as a Coed (often called Jack and Jill) Meet. College Timbersports teams began appearing in the 1940's with the Northeast of the US having the highest concentration of them. That's all the history I know, and I will get more up here as soon as I find a Historian.
